Nature's Little Alchemists Magically Turning CO₂, Light and N₂ into Alkanes and Protein Gold

In terms of the selection of strains, we recorded the culture conditions and metabolic pathways of different strains through the BACDIVE database and Web of Science literature search, and screened out strains that met our requirements and had symbiotic potential.

Physiological conditions and metabolic pathways of all the above strains were summarized, and strains with similar growth conditions and complementary metabolic pathways were selected, in order to find the most symbiotic potential as our chassis cells. After preliminary comparison, we selected Rolstonia, microalgae as carbon fixation module, and Vickers as nitrogen fixation module. We conducted a deeper investigation on these bacteria and selected several subspecies with excellent properties:
